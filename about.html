杰威爾音樂

JVR Music
JVR Music Co., Ltd. is derived from the transliteration of the English name JVR Music, and JVR is the combination of the English abbreviations of Jay Chou (Jay), Fang Wenshan (Vincent) and Yang Junrong (JR), who have been partners for many years. If Jay Chou insists on the quality of music, this golden team of the "Golden Triangle" will continue to lead many fans to travel in the infinite world of audio and video!
