Jay Chou ALBUMS 2000-2022

2000 "JAY"

Jay is the debut studio album by Taiwanese singer Jay Chou, released on 7 November 2000 by BMG Taiwan. The album was nominated for five Golden Melody Awards and won for Best Pop Vocal Album. The album also won an IFPI Hong Kong Top Sales Music Award for Top 10 Best Selling Mandarin Albums of the Year. The track, "Adorable Lady", is listed at number 74 on the 2000s Hit FM Top 100 Singles of the Year chart. The track, "Starry Mood", is also listed at number 80 on the 2001's chart.

2001 "FANTASY"

Fantasy is the second studio album, released on 14 September 2001 by BMG Taiwan. The album was nominated for ten Golden Melody Awards and won five awards, including Best Pop Vocal Album, Best Album Producer, and Best Composer. The album also won for an IFPI Hong Kong Top Sales Music Award for Top 10 Best Selling Mandarin Albums of the Year. The tracks, "Simple Love" and "I Find It Hard to Say", are listed at number 2 and number 17 respectively on the 2001's Hit FM Top 100 Singles of the Year chart. Click on image to see all the tracks in Fantasy

2001 "THE EIGHT DIMENSIONS"

The Eight Dimensions is the third studio album, released on 18 July 2002 by BMG Taiwan. The album was nominated for five Golden Melody Awards. The album also won for an IFPI Hong Kong Top Sales Music Award for Best Selling Mandarin Album of the Year. The tracks, "Secret Sign", "Back to the Past", and "The Final Battle", are listed at number 1, number 5, and number 42 respectively on the 2002's Hit FM Top 100 Singles of the Year chart.
Click on image to see all the tracks in The Eight Dimension !

2003 "YE HUI-MEI"

Yeh Hui-Mei is the fourth studio album, released on 29 July 2003 by Alfa Music. The album was named after his mother. The album was nominated for eight Golden Melody Awards and won two awards, including Best Pop Vocal Album and Best Music Video for "Class 3-2". The album also won an IFPI Hong Kong Top Sales Music Award for Best Selling Mandarin Album of the Year. The tracks, "Sunny Day", "Her Eyelashes", and "East Wind Breaks", are listed at number 2, number 15, and number 41 respectively on the 2003's Hit FM Top 100 Singles of the Year chart. Click on image to see all the tracks in Ye Hui Mei

2004 "COMMON JASMINE ORANGE"

Common Jasmine Orange is the fifth studio album, released on 3 August 2004 by Sony Music Taiwan. The album was nominated for six Golden Melody Awards. The album won for an IFPI Hong Kong Top Sales Music Award for Best Selling Mandarin Album of the Year. According to IFPI, The album is also listed at number 43 of 2004's global best-selling albums. The tracks, "Common Jasmine Orange", "Excuse", and "Wounds of War", are listed at number 1, number 10, and number 88 respectively on the 2004's Hit FM Top 100 Singles of the Year chart.
Click on image to see all the tracks in Common Jasmine Orange!

2005 "NOVENMBER's CHOPIN"

Novenmber's Chopin is the sixth studio album, released on 1 November 2005 by Sony Music Taiwan. The album was nominated for two Golden Melody Awards. The album won for an IFPI Hong Kong Top Sales Music Award for Top 10 Best Selling Mandarin Albums of the Year. The track, "Drifting", was nominated a Golden Horse Award for Best Original Film Song and a Hong Kong Film Award for Best Original Film Song. Click on image to see all the tracks in Novenmber's Chopin

2006 "STILL FANTASY"

Still Fantasy is the seventh studio album, released on 5 September 2006 by Sony Music Taiwan & Alfa Music. The album was nominated for three Golden Melody Awards. The album also won an IFPI Hong Kong Top Sales Music Award for Best Selling Mandarin Album of the Year. The track, "Chrysanthemum Terrace," won a Hong Kong Film Award for Best Original Film Song. The tracks, "Far Away," "Listen to Mom," and "White Windmill," are listed at number 2, number 8 and number 53 respectively on 2006's Hit FM Top Singles of the Year chart. Click on image to see all the tracks in Still Fantasy

2007 "ON THE RUN!"

On the Run! is the eighth studio album, released on 1 November 2007 by JVR Music. The album was nominated for five Golden Melody Awards and won Song of the Year, Best Composer, and Best Lyricist for "Blue and White Porcelain". The album also won an IFPI Hong Kong Top Sales Music Award for Top 10 Best Selling Mandarin Album of the Year. The tracks, "Cowboy is Very Busy", "Rainbow", and "Sunshine Nerd", are listed at number 11, number 28, and number 59 respectively on the 2010s Hit FM Top 100 Singles of the Year chart. Click on image to see all the tracks in On the Run!

2008 "CAPRICORN"

Capricorn is the ninth studio album, released on 14 October 2008 by JVR Music. At the 20th Golden Melody Awards, the album was nominated for eight awards, winning Best mandarin Male Singer, Song of the Year for "Fragrant Rice", Best Music Video for "Magician". The tracks, "Fragrant Rice", "Give Me the Time of a Song", and "The Promised Love", are listed at number 3, number 17, and number 56 respectively on the 2008's Hit FM Top 100 Singles of the Year chart. Click on image to see all the tracks in Capricorn!

2010 "THE ERA"

The Era is the tenth studio album, released on 18 May 2010 by JVR Music and Sony Music Taiwan. The album was nominated for six Golden Melody Awards and won for Best Mandarin Male Singer, Best Mandarin album, and Best Musical Arrangement for "Free Tutorial Video". The tracks, "Superman Can't Fly", "Rain Falls All Night", and "The Era", are listed at number 2, number 10, and number 39 respectively on the 2010s Hit FM Top 100 Singles of the Year chart. Wow! or known as Exclamation Point is the eleventh studio album, released on 11 November 2011 by JVR Music.The album was nominated for three Golden Melody Awards and won for Best Album Packaging. Click on image to see all the tracks in WOW!

2012 "Opus 12"

Opus 12, released on 28 December 2012 by JVR Music. The album was nominated for four Golden Melody Awards. Click on image to see all the tracks in Opus 12.

2014 "Aiyo, Not Bad"

Aiyo, Not Bad is the thirteenth studio album, released on 26 December 2014 by Sony Music Taiwan and JVR Music. The album was nominated for three Golden Melody Awards. Click on image to see all the tracks in Aiyo, Not Bad.

2016 "Jay Chou's Bedtime Stories"

Jay Chou's Bedtime Stories is the fourteenth studio album, released on 24 June 2016 by JVR Music. The album was nominated for three Golden Melody Awards. Click on image to see all the tracks in Jay Chou's Bedtime Stories.

2022 "Greatest Works of Art"

Greatest Works of Art is the fifteenth studio album by Taiwanese singer Jay Chou, released on 15 July 2022 through JVR Music. It is Chou's first album since his fourteenth studio album Jay Chou's Bedtime Stories and was preceded by five singles released from 2018 to 2022—"Waiting for You" with Gary Yang, "If You Don't Love Me, It's Fine", "Won't Cry" with Mayday vocalist Ashin, "Mojito" and the title track. The song "I Truly Believe" was released in 2019 as the theme for the 2019 Chinese action film Skyfire. The title track and "Still Wandering" also had music videos shot for them, which were released in July 2022. Click on image to see all the tracks in Greatest Works of Art.
